How Government Uses in IT Companies

The government uses IT companies to improve public services, digitize records, and implement tech-based governance. These companies develop portals, manage data, provide cybersecurity, and help in executing smart city, healthcare, and education projects. IT companies act like technology partners for the government, helping it work faster, safer, and smarter.

Purpose Example / Description IT Companies Involved
Government Project Execution Develop portals like Income Tax, Passport Seva, etc. Infosys, TCS, Wipro
Cybersecurity & Data Protection Protect sensitive government data and infrastructure CDAC, HCL, TCS
Digitization of Records Convert physical records into digital format (land, Aadhaar, etc.) Wipro, NIC, TCS
Smart City & Infrastructure Tech systems for traffic, waste, and urban planning Tech Mahindra, IBM, L&T Infotech
Healthcare Services e-Hospitals, digital health ID, COVID portals Wipro, NIC, HCL
Education & Skill Portals Platforms like SWAYAM, National Digital Library Persistent Systems, HCL
AI & Analytics Predicting outcomes, analyzing data (farming, health, economy) TCS, IBM, Accenture
