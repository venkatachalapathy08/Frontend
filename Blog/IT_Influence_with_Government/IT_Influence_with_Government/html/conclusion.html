CONCLUSION

The influence of IT companies on government operations is a double-edged sword. On one side, it brings immense benefits like faster public services, greater transparency, modern technologies, and efficient data handling. It plays a vital role in supporting national missions like Digital India and Smart Cities, while also creating new job and skill development opportunities. However, this collaboration must be handled with caution. If IT companies gain too much control or influence, it could lead to privacy violations, biased policies, overdependence, and even national security risks. To balance the pros and cons, the government must ensure strict regulations, transparent partnerships, and ethical practices in all collaborations with IT firms.
